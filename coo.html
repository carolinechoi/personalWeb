'510

I designed and coded this app to turn my city's citizens into more active and passionate Alamedans. The app is called '510 in honour of the Bay's area code.


As a hybrid mobile app, '510 uses Firebase for auth & data storage. Languages used: HTML, SCSS, Angular Typescript

Using '510, Alameda community members are able to post opportunities for volunteering, such as beach cleanups or picking fruit from our community garden. Users can then sign-up to volunteer at these events. Anyone can download the app for use, but can also join as an organisation.



Primarily, the objective is to create a more environmentally sustainable community by the year of 2020.
