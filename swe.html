Harry Potter Geo-Clock

After eight Saturdays of working with UC Berkeley's amazing Society of Women Engineers, I built a final project with my freshly acquired knowledge.


In a group with three other bright high school girls, I led the team by coding and designing. With only four days to hack a functioning product, I learned much about teamwork and efficiency.

"But she may already know, that excellent clock of hers."

Our “Harry Potter Clock” uses geo-location and geo-fencing services to locate your family and friends.
