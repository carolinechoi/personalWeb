Untitled.

This app, I created after looking for minimalistic to-do-list apps - I didn't find any that fit my desired aesthetic. So during my spare time, I coded this app and pieced together the functions. It's a hybrid mobile app that uses Firebase as a database.


Languages used: HTML, SCSS, Angular Typescript

Usage is very straightforward. A user logs in and creates to-do-list "groups". Within these groups, a user can add various to-do items that can be checked off when completed.


While I intended this app for my purposes, my friends and family have offered positive feedback, which makes me optimistic about my app's future.
